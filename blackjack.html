Your goal in Blackjack is to get as close as you can to the value 21, while also getting a higher value than the opponent. To play, you will first need to click the "Deal" button. To add an extra card to your hand, click the 'hit' button. Once you feel like you are close to 21, click the 'stand' button. Clicking 'stand' will reveal the opponent's facedown card. If your card values are greater than the opponent's, you win! If your opponent's card values are greater than yours, you lose.

Note: ACE cards always hold the value 11.
